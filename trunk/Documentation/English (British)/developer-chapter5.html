Chapter 5: Export Format Module

The subroutines listed here in this chapter are used by the export module. The export module allows a scanned document to be transferred to the desired medium or sent through the internet. (such as Download and Email).

An example of an export module is the Download module (which makes the scanned document downloadable through a web browser after processing).

When the export module is loaded when the processing or the available settings are being obtained, they are available from the $xestiascan_exportmodule.
